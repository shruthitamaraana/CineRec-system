How CineRec Works

A peek under the hood of our Hybrid Recommendation Engine.

1. Content Analysis

We use TF-IDF vectors and cosine similarity to deeply analyze movie metadata, genres, and plot descriptions.

2. Collaborative Filtering

Our algorithm looks at complex user rating matrices to find patterns among audiences with similar tastes to yours.

3. Hybrid Scoring

We merge both models, intelligently weighting the scores to eliminate the "cold start" problem and deliver pinpoint accuracy.

Why We Created CineRec

We've all been there: spending 45 minutes scrolling through streaming platforms, overwhelmed by choices, only to end up re-watching a show from 10 years ago.

Existing algorithms often trap you in an "echo chamber" of the same blockbusters. We built CineRec to break that cycle. By implementing a hybrid architecture, our goal is to help users discover hidden gems and expand their cinematic horizons without the endless scrolling fatigue.
